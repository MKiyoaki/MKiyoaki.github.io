It was a privilege to present my research at the Google DeepMind UK Education event this past week. As a participant in the Research Ready program, I was proud to showcase my work, and I'm thrilled it was met with such positive appreciation.

The day was filled with insightful conversations with inspiring researchers, industry leaders, and the impressive cohort of students in the program. Witnessing the range of innovative projects, from healthcare to sustainability, was a powerful reminder that we are all shaping a brighter future.

I'm immensely grateful for the opportunity and for the amazing colleagues I've had the privilege to work with. The connections made here will be central to my future development, and I eagerly look forward to the opportunities ahead.
